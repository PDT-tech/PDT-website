Who we are

Men who love to sing —
and bring that joy to
their community

Portland DayTime Singers is a men's barbershop chorus that performs sing-outs for community events, senior centers, and independent and assisted living communities throughout greater Portland. We rehearse Monday mornings and welcome any man who loves music and is willing to learn.

We are proud Lodge #18 of the Worldwide Barbershop Quartet Association.
