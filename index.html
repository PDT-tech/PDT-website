Who we are

Men who love to sing — and sing for those who need it most

Portland DayTime Singers is a men's barbershop chorus that performs free concerts for seniors and residents of care facilities throughout greater Portland. We rehearse Monday mornings and welcome men of all experience levels — no audition required.

We are proud Lodge #18 of the Worldwide Barbershop Quartet Association.
