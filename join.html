Join Us

Come Sing With Us

No audition required — just a love of singing

What to expect

Show up. Sing. Belong.

Your first visit is easy. Walk in, introduce yourself, and we'll find you a spot in the section that suits your voice. You don't need to read music — most of our members learned their parts by ear.

We rehearse for two hours each Monday morning, working on our repertoire of barbershop standards and community concert material. There's always time for conversation, coffee, and a bit of camaraderie before and after.

Membership dues are modest. Come to a rehearsal first — there's no commitment until you're sure it's for you.

The "audition"

Just sing Happy Birthday

Sing Happy Birthday in whatever key feels natural. That one song tells us your range, your pitch sense, and your comfort level — everything we need to find you the right section.

If you love to sing, you belong here. We're not looking for perfection — we're looking for enthusiasm and commitment to the craft.

Rehearsals

Every Monday morning

When
Mondays, 10:30 am – 12:30 pm
We meet weekly year-round, with occasional breaks around major holidays. The schedule is announced in advance through our member communications.
What to bring
Just yourself
We'll provide a name tag, sheet music, and a warm welcome. Wear comfortable clothes — we don't stand on ceremony at rehearsal.
Where
Westside Journey UMC
13420 SW Butner Rd
Beaverton, OR 97005

Free parking on site. Accessible entrance available. The chorus meets in the main fellowship hall.
First visit?
Let us know you're coming
Dropping a quick note to info@pdtsingers.org before your first rehearsal helps us make sure someone is there to greet you.

Ready?

The door is open

Whether you're a seasoned singer or someone who's always wanted to try — come find out what barbershop harmony is all about. We'll see you Monday morning.
