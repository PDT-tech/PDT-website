Stay in the loop

Follow along, spread the word

The best way to stay connected with Portland DayTime Singers is to drop us a note at info@pdtsingers.org and ask to be added to our Friends list. We send occasional emails about upcoming performances and chorus news — no spam, ever.

If you've seen us perform and would like to support what we do, the best support is simply telling people about us. More ears in the audience, and more voices on the risers, are the greatest gifts you can give a chorus.
