Who we are

A chorus born from a love of singing and service

Founded by Director Chris Gable and Grand Poohbah Kevin Bier, Portland DayTime Singers brings men together on Monday mornings to rehearse and perform the barbershop tradition — close-harmony singing at its warmest and most human.

We are proud Lodge #18 of the Worldwide Barbershop Quartet Association, and we welcome men of all experience levels — from seasoned barbershoppers to those who've never sung in a group before.

Barbershop Tradition

Singing for Seniors
Free concerts at care facilities and senior centers throughout Portland — twice a month, and more during Christmastime.
☀️
Daytime Hours
Monday morning rehearsals — designed for men whose schedules work better during the day.

Come sing with us

No audition. Just a song.

We don't audition — we do a friendly voice placement. Sing Happy Birthday for us in whatever key feels natural, and we'll know exactly how to make you part of the sound. If you love to sing, you belong here.

Getting started
Just show up — or reach out first
Come to any Monday rehearsal, or drop us a note and we'll be in touch before your first visit.

"Sing Happy Birthday wherever it's comfortable in your range. That tells us everything we need to know about how to help you find your voice in our sound."

— Chris Gable, Director
