Friends of PDT Singers

We'd love to stay connected

You don't have to sing to be part of what we're building. Friends of PDT Singers are the people who cheer us on, show up to our sing-outs, and help spread the word about Barbershop harmony in the Portland community.

If that sounds like you, we'd love to stay connected.
