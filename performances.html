What to expect

A free, joyful hour of live music

Our performances are warm, informal, and interactive. We sing a mix of classic barbershop favorites, American standards, and popular songs — all in four-part a cappella harmony.

We love singing for our audiences as much as they love hearing us. Expect some friendly banter, audience participation, and more than a few smiles.

All performances are free of charge. We bring the music — you bring the enthusiasm.
