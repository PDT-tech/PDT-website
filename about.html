About Us

Who We Are

A men's barbershop chorus serving Portland since 2025

Our Mission

Music, Fellowship & Fun

Portland DayTime Singers exists to bring the joy of barbershop harmony to our community. We perform free concerts at senior care facilities, community centers, and public events throughout greater Portland — bringing live music to people who might not otherwise have it.

We believe that singing together builds connection. Our chorus is a place where men of all ages and backgrounds come together every Monday morning to make music, make friends, and make a difference.

No musical experience is required to join us — only a love of singing and a willingness to show up. We'll take care of the rest.

Repertoire
Barbershop & beyond

Membership
Open to all men

Leadership

The people who make it happen

Music Director
Chris Gabel
Chris leads our weekly rehearsals and shapes the musical direction of the chorus. He brings deep experience in barbershop arranging and choral conducting.
Grand Poohbah & President
Kevin Bier
Kevin oversees the chorus's operations, social events, and community partnerships. The Grand Poohbah is our chief of fellowship — the glue that holds us together.

Affiliation

WBQA Lodge #18

Portland DayTime Singers is proud to be Lodge #18 of the Worldwide Barbershop Quartet Association. The WBQA supports barbershop choruses and quartets worldwide, fostering the tradition of close-harmony a cappella singing.

As a WBQA lodge, our members have access to contest opportunities, educational resources, and a global network of fellow barbershoppers.
